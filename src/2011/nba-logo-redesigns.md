[Michael Weinstein](http://www.michael-weinstein.com/) is putting some modern graphical design elements to the [NBA logos](http://www.michael-weinstein.com/2010/11/nba-team-logo-redesigns/). From what I understand, he's off to do every NBA team, so let's cheer him along. Many of these designs are pretty great and each NBA team should seriously consider whether or not to use one of his suggested designs.Â  Most notable (at this point), the L.A. Clippers and the soon-to-be Brooklyn Nets. 

I think I like the [Sacramento King's 'Alternate' logo](http://www.michael-weinstein.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/04/mountain_lion.jpg) better than his proposed design. The suggested design looks way too Barclay's Premiere League to me.
